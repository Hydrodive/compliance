Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account details such as email address and login credentials, which are required for authentication and access to the application

  • Basic usage data necessary to maintain application stability, security, and performance

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate users and manage user accounts
  • To store, retrieve, and display user-specific event reports linked to the authenticated account
  • To enable access to user data across multiple devices after login
  • To maintain and improve app functionality
  • To communicate important service-related updates

Account Requirement

This application is fully account-based. All user data is stored on remote servers and is accessible only after authentication. The app does not support guest access, anonymous usage, or local-only data storage. An account is required for the application to function.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Data may only be shared if required by law or when necessary to protect the safety, security, or integrity of users and the application.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us through the appropriate support channels.
